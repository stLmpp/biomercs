Hello, I'm stLmpp.

I play The Mercenaries since Resident Evil 4. Peaked at Resident Evil 5 and 6, but always had the same problem as everyone else, the leaderboards (when existent) were full of cheaters.

For a long time, the community used to have a sheet with all scores from Resident Evil 5, but it was not that updated, and had a lot of flaws, because there were no (explicit) regulations and the updates were dependent on some people.

So I had the idea of creating this site, to keep a clean and legit leaderboards, and add support for others Resident Evil games too.

Resident Evil Village recently (2021) launched, and there's no in-game leaderboards, so I saw an opportunity to launch the site.

search There a link in the header ( add Submit score ), that redirects you to the page of score submission.
There you can select all the parameters of your score (Platform, Game, Stage, etc), and submit it. It depends.

If your score is Solo:
  1. It goes to the approval of an admin
  2. If an admin approves your score: What happens when my score is approved by an admin?
  3. If an admin rejects your score: What happens when my score is rejected by an admin?
  4. If an admin requests changes to your score: What do I need to do when an admin request a change in my score?
If your score is Duo:
  1. It goes to the approval of your partner
  2. If your partner approves your score: What happens when my score is approved by my patner?
  3. If your partner rejects your score: What happens when my score is rejected by my partner?
  4. If your partner requests changes to your score: What do I need to do when my partner request a change in my score?
